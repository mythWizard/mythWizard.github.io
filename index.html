Biography

I am a software engineer on leave of absence from the College of William and Mary’s M.S./Ph.D. program in computer science in order to pursue industry work.

I graduated summa cum laude from Longwood University in 2019 with a B.S. in computer science and a minor in chemistry. At Longwood, I served as the president of the university’s chapter of UPE and as vice president of ACM.

In my free time, I enjoy (re)creating video games, specifically arcade games.
